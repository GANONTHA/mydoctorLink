Who are we ?

MyDoctorLink is a platform that has the unique goal of connecting rural communities to doctors. We believe that distance should not be an obstacle nor a barriere to access good healthcare.

On our platform, we have range of several qualified and experienced doctors in nearly all domaines. Our doctors are ready to connect with you tackle your health issue. Our telemedice is specified in deliverying first and prelimany diagnoses to our patients and patient monitoring after treatment.

We support rural communities though our services.

Our primary targets are children and women who are the most affected population. Our services include, 90% off of charge treatment, free diagnosis, free patient monoring and 50% off the cost of medicines.

We are supported by a wide community of donators. You can contribute now and save lives.
